512 Rustic Drive

The project is subjected to finish by the end of 2022.

This is a 2635 sq ft, 3 bd, 3.5 bath open concept floor plan with canyon views from main living areas and open terrace backyard from master bedroom & open fireplace . The house has a 2-car garage at floor level, main living/kitchen/dining area as open space in second level and three well-sized bedrooms with walk in closets, each with their own bath upstairs. The house was created with a modern design aesthetic in mind. Currently under construction.
